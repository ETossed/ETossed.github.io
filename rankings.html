ETossed's Melee Top 100

Introduction

I figured I'd make my own ranking because I was gonna do a top 100 ballot whether or not I ended up getting an ballot at the end of year, because I love ranking and tiering things.

Anyways, here's the methodology I used:

    Losses at locals don't matter, wins do! Generally I stray away from using local data but in certain cases it's helpful (please attend your locals)
    Placements don't matter except for first and maybe second
    H2Hs matter a lot
    Good wins valued more than bad losses, especially in dominant H2Hs
    Tried to value attendance
    Using exact same eligibility list as SSBMRank 2022
I designed and coded this website from scratch (There's even lightmode in the top right corner, which I assume only DarkGenex will use), the accent color is random (refresh the page for fun), and the source code is linked at the bottom of the webpage. This is not an algorithmic ranking. This is what my ballot would have been. If you have any questions please direct them to my twitter which is linked at the bottom of the page.

The first section is for a few players that I specifically wanted to highlight who didn't meet activity requirements but would have most likely made top 100, the second section is honorable mentions for those who just barely missed the top 100, then the section after is the top 100.
